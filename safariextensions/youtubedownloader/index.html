YouTubeDownloader

YouTubeDownloader is a Safari Extension for YouTube.

Once installed, YouTubeDownloader adds a 'Download Video' button to every YouTube watch page:

To use YouTubeDownloader, you must have Safari 5 or newer, and you must enable extensions.

YouTubeDownloader.safariextz
To download, right-click the link above and choose 'Save Linked File...'

YouTubeDownloader is free software. Happy Downloading!
